# **App Name**: VoiceStream Academy

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Display a persuasive headline and call-to-action to engage visitors.
- Content Blocks: Organize textual content into cards and lists of benefits for easy reading.
- Bonus Highlights: Showcase special bonuses with visual cues to attract attention.
- Pricing Section: Present pricing details with a prominent conversion button.
- Footer: Provide institutional information and support contact in a simple footer.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant green (#28A745) for CTA buttons to signal conversion and trust.
- Background color: Light grey (#F8F9FA) to provide a neutral backdrop ensuring readability and focus on content.
- Accent color: Dark grey (#343A40) for text, providing a high contrast with the light background improving legibility.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a modern and clean aesthetic, optimized for digital screens. Suitable for both titles and body text.
- Mobile-first, responsive grid layout adapting across breakpoints at 480px, 768px, 1024px and 1440px for consistent experience on all devices.
- Use arrow (➡️) and checkmark (✅) icons to highlight benefits and action items within the content blocks.
- Subtle hover effects on buttons providing visual feedback to user interactions and improve UX.
